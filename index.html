About Projects Résumé

Whether expanding machine learning models of humor detection or investigating practical issues surrounding healthcare and voting practices, I'm excited about computational approaches to human behavior and the systems that govern it.

I am currently researching how novel experimental methods can reveal the development of categorization with Daphna Buchsbaum at the Computational Cognitive Development Lab, and am also working with Rohan Alexander on an open source R Package containing lessons on R essentials. I have previously spent time studying the effect of nurse-staffing payment models on avoidable hospitalizations at RTI International.
