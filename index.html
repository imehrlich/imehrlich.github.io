About Projects Resume

I'm excited by using data for interpretable and informative storytelling, especially about human behavior and interaction. My projects include models to predict sound-color pairings for an individual with synesthesia and increasing the accuracy of multisensory humor detection.

I am currently working as a Data Scientist and Software Engineer at Composable Analytics, designing and applying tools for intelligent data analytics in operational settings. I'm also working with UofT Faculty to develop an open source R Package with lessons on the R programming language. I previously spent time at the Computational Cognitive Development Lab researching methods to interpret high dimensional data and the development of categorization.
